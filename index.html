Maindang NHS Joins DepEd’s 236K Tree Drive

On December 6, 2023, the teaching personnel of Maindang National High School, alongside the SSLG and YES-O Federation Officers, actively participated in the Department of Education’s tree planting initiative, “236K – A Christmas Gift for the Children.” The event was held at the SHS site in Agdahon, Cuartero, Capiz.
